Map Guide

How to Use This Map

  1. Explore localities. Larger, darker circles indicate more records. Select a circle or a locality in the right pane to see its records and links to the Library of Virginia online catalog.
  2. Refine the records. Choose a subject or adjust either end of the year range. The map, counts, and locality list update together.
  3. Sort the locality list. The right pane begins with the largest record count. Use the sort menu to arrange localities alphabetically.
  4. Compare historical boundaries. Turn on Historical boundaries to draw county lines for the final year in the selected range. The circles themselves represent the collection’s modern locality assignments, so the overlay is a historical reference rather than a one-to-one match.
