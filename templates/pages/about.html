Reading the record county by county

Records of Revolution gathers legislative petitions, declarations made by veterans and their families in support of Revolutionary War pensions, and related court records. Claims, protests, disputes, and sworn accounts are arranged here by the counties associated with them.

Each available document links to its Library of Virginia online catalog record, where digitized materials may be viewed. The Catalogue, Map, and Search offer different ways to encounter the same body of records.

Records and online catalog maintained by the Library of Virginia.
